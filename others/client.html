Our Story

Our story began in 2022 when our founder, a college student studying Mechanical Engineering, saw a need for quality corporate housing in Greensboro, NC. Determined to fill this gap in the market, they started our company with just 1 units. Over the years, we have grown and now manage over 10 units, always looking to expand and provide even more options for our clients. Our founder's background in engineering has helped us to ensure that our properties are not only stylish and comfortable, but also well-maintained and functional. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and a home away from home for our clients, and we are excited to continue growing and serving the community in Greensboro and beyond.
